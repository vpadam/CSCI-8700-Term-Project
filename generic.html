Help Page

Information page about the Website

WHAT is it about

The application is purposed to provide parking availability by giving an access to the aerial view of UNO campus parking. The driver can book the available slot by interacting with the user already parked in the spot and ready to leave. This gives an easy way to find parking spots and reduces the traffic around UNO campus.

WHO can access the application

All students and faculty members of UNO can access the application with their valid NUID or UNO email address.

HOW to sign-in

Users can access the application by signing-up with their UNO-NUID and password. An account is created which is unique for each NUID.

WHY to use this application

Apart from having the access to the open parking spots, this application helps to save a dollar by giving your spot to someone searching for it.

WHEN can you request a refund

  • if the transaction fails
  • if the parking you booked is not provided
  • if you booked it by accident (accidental purchase refunds)
  • if somebody else is using your account without your consent (unauthorized purchase refunds)
